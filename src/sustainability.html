Sustainability

Sustainable eating is about making food choices that are respectful to the environment while keeping our bodies healthy. Some foods are kinder to the planet than others. By choosing to eat foods that have a smaller carbon footprint, we can take action against the climate crisis and keep Earth a home for future generations.

Eating sustainably is about thinking about where food comes from and making choices accordingly!

Local
Foods

Think about an orange, a yummy snack. Where did the orange come from? Let's think about two oranges, orange A is from your local farmers market versus orange B from industrial agriculture. Orange A is most likely produced in your neighborhood by people in your community. Orange B could be grown hundreds of miles away and had to be shipped a long distance to make it to your hand. Orange A has a smaller carbon footprint then Orange B because it requires less energy and less harmful emissions to be enjoyed.
